How Generative AI is
Transforming Ship Design
Date: Tuesday, April 14, 2026

Could AI design the ship of the future faster than traditional methods?

Key advantages include:
• Faster design cycles – multiple iterations can be generated in minutes.
• Improved hydrodynamics for lower resistance and better performance.
• Reduced fuel consumption through more efficient hull forms.
• Data-backed decisions based on real simulations and performance analysis.
• Streamlined design workflows with less trial and error.

These tools help naval architects evaluate many design options, compare performance automatically, and reach suitable configurations more quickly.

TMS AITECH 2026 will explore how shipyards, engineering firms, and technology providers are adopting Generative AI to enhance accuracy, speed, and cost efficiency in ship design and development.
